# Privacy Policy for KeySmith

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

KeySmith does not collect, store, or transmit any personal 
data or usage information. All data remains on your device.

No accounts are required. No analytics are used. 
No third-party services receive any data from this app.

## Data Storage

All site profiles are stored locally on your device only. 
Exported backup files are saved to a location you choose 
and are encrypted with a passphrase you provide.

## Third Party Links

The app contains a link to Ko-fi (ko-fi.com) for optional 
donations. Visiting that link is subject to Ko-fi's own 
privacy policy.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, 
please open an issue on the KeySmith GitHub repository.
